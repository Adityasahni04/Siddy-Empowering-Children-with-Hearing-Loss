Transforming Lives Through Specialized Support

Children with prelingual or congenital hearing loss face unique challenges in developing communication and language skills. These challenges often lead to difficulties in learning, social interaction, and inclusion, potentially hindering their mental growth and development.

At Siddy, we address this critical need by providing specialized programs that help these children overcome communication barriers. Without proper support, a significant portion of our population risks being marginalized and unable to fully participate in society. Our mission is to change this narrative through early intervention and comprehensive support.
